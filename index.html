i'm nisarga,

nice to meet you!

I took the long way here. I started as an engineer, initially drawn to systems, logic, and how things fit together. Somewhere along the way, I realised the problems I actually wanted to solve were human ones, basically how brands communicate, how stories land, and how content moves people to act. So I crossed over, and I never looked back.

Now I build marketing like I used to build products and services with structure, data, and a lot of intention. I've led campaigns across B2B SaaS, DTC, and nonprofit spaces and what drives me is content that doesn't just perform on a dashboard but actually resonates. The kind that makes someone stop scrolling, feel something, and remember you.

I believe the best marketing is really just great storytelling with a strategy behind it. Every brand has something worth saying, I'm obsessed with finding the sharpest, most honest way to say it, and then making sure the right people hear it.

I'm looking for a team where I can build things that are measurable and meaningful.
